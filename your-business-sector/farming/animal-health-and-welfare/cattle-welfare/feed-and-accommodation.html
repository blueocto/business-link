Cattle welfare

Feed and accommodation

All cattle need a balanced daily diet to maintain full health and energy. You should plan any changes in the diet and introduce them gradually.

Feed, water and other substances

Find the Department for Environment, Food and Rural Affairs' (Defra's) guidance on diet and water requirements for cattle on the Agricultural Document Library (AdLib) website - Opens in a new window.

Accommodation

You should seek appropriate welfare advice when constructing new buildings or modifying existing ones. Advice on accommodation for cattle can be found in the Defra welfare code where guidance is split into the following areas;

Straw yards

Find Defra's guidance on cleaning and managing straw yards on the AdLib website - Opens in a new window.

Cublicles

Find Defra's cubicle installation guidance on the AdLib website - Opens in a new window.

Cowsheds

Find Defra's guidance on cowshed management on the AdLib website - Opens in a new window.

Space allowances

Find Defra's guidance on space allowances on the AdLib website - Opens in a new window.

Ventilation

Find Defra's ventilation requirements for all cattle on the AdLib website - Opens in a new window.

Lighting

Find Defra's recommended indoor lighting guidance on the AdLib website - Opens in a new window.

Calculate the statutory redundancy pay due to your employee

Introduction

An employee being made redundant with two or more years' continuous service has the right to receive:

  • a statutory redundancy payment
  • a written statement setting out the payment amount and how it was calculated

You can use our interactive tool to calculate the statutory redundancy payment due to your employee and create a personalised written statement. This can be saved on your own system or given straight to the employee.

This tool is based on the statutory scheme and does not apply to employers who offer redundancy payments over the statutory maximum.

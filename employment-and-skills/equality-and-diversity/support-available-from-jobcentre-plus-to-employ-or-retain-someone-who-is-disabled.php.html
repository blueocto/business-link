Support available from Jobcentre Plus to employ or retain someone who is disabled

Introduction

Employers who actively seek applications from disabled people have a much wider choice of potential employees, with a good range of skills and positive attitude towards work.

Studies show that disabled people in work tend to have better attendance records, stay with employers longer and have fewer accidents at work. Showing a positive approach towards disability tends to foster good relations with other staff and can enhance your reputation as a good employer.

Jobcentre Plus can provide you with specialist support to help you recruit and retain disabled workers through a number of measures such as:

  • a Disability Employment Adviser who can offer help and advice about the range of specialist support available and advice on the wording of your vacancies to ensure you get people with the right skills
  • Access to Work
  • The Disability Symbol
  • Work Choice

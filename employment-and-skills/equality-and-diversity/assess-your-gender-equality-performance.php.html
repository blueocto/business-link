Assess your gender equality performance

Introduction

This tool will help you identify where your working practices could be changed to ensure that men and women are treated fairly and have equal opportunities in your business.

You will be asked questions about your recruitment practices, training and development, work-life balance and written equality policies.

If you do not have this information to hand, you should consider looking into these areas of your business before starting the tool.

Start using the tool.

It should take no longer than five minutes to answer the short series of questions about your working practices and staff.

You can save your answers at any time and return to them later if you choose.

At the end of the tool, you will receive tailored information and advice, which you can save to refer to later.

Managing paternity and adoption leave and pay

Introduction

Employers must not only be aware of the rights of new and expectant mothers on their workforce, but also those of fathers and people looking to adopt a child.

Using our two interactive tools, you can find out your employee's entitlement to ordinary paternity leave and statutory adoption leave, how much you should pay them and your obligations when they return to work.

These tools do not cover additional paternity leave - for more information see our guide on additional paternity leave entitlement.

Each tool should take no more than five minutes to complete.

Please choose one of the following options to begin using the tools:

  • Ordinary paternity leave, rights and pay
  • Statutory adoption leave, rights and pay

You can also use our interactive tool to find out how to manage new and expectant mothers in your workplace.

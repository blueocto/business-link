Advertise a job and Jobcentre Plus Services

Introduction

Advertising your job with Jobcentre Plus can save you time and money as well as providing access to services that can help you recruit the right person for your job.

You can tell Jobcentre Plus about your job vacancy either online, by telephone or by email. See the page in this guide on how to advertise your job.

Thousands of potential recruits can then access your vacancy via our national network of touch screen terminals, our job-matching telephone service and on the Directgov website.

From late Autumn, DWP will launch Universal Jobmatch, a new job posting and matching service. See our page DWP launch new job posting and matching service for more information.

If you are a small business you can use our dedicated helpline for advice about recruitment, employment related matters or to advertise a job. See the page in this guide on Jobcentre Plus services to support small businesses.

This guide explains the options available to you when you choose to advertise your jobs with Jobcentre Plus.

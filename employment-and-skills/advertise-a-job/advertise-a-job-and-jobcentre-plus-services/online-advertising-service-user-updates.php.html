News and updates about the Jobcentre Plus, Employer Direct online advertising service.

Universal Jobmatch is coming late Autumn - DWP launch new job posting and matching service.

Improvements to the way your job vacancies are displayed

Jobcentre Plus has improved the matching of jobs to customers' search criteria and increased the number of vacancies that are returned in a jobsearch enquiry.

If you notify a new job vacancy to Employer Direct online or Employer Direct, it will now be displayed within a few minutes. This is subject to your job vacancy meeting the Jobcentre Plus vacancy criteria.

If you amend or close any job vacancy the Jobs and Skills website will be updated every 60 minutes during business hours - 07.00 to 18.00, Monday to Friday.

Advertising your vacancies nationally

Please do not use the 'All GB' indicator.

Employers wishing to advetise their vacancy 'nationally' e.g. home based' vacancies that are not location specific, should select individual 'Region' indicators rather than selecting the 'All GB' box.

Equality Act 2010

The Equality Act 2010 came into force on 1 October 2010.

Find out more about the Equality Act and its application on the Equality and Human Rights Commission website.

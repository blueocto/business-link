Advertise a job and Jobcentre Plus Services

Using the Jobcentre Plus online advertising service

Employer Direct online (EDon) is the current Jobcentre Plus vacancy advertising service that allows you to notify your jobs online.

Universal Jobmatch is a new online job posting and matching service and will launch in late Autumn. See our page DWP launch new job posting and matching service for more information. You should continue to use the existing services in the meantime.

The key benefits to your business of using this service are:

  • it's easy to use, saving you time and money - you can notify your job vacancies straight from your computer, via the internet, 24 hours a day 7 days a week
  • you'll have wide access to a pool of potential recruits - your job vacancy is displayed within a few minutes on the Jobs and Skills jobsearch on Directgov - the UK's most visited website - subject to validation
  • immediate access to people who are looking for work - your job vacancy is displayed on touch screen terminals in all Jobcentre Plus offices
  • saving time by managing your vacancies online, at any time when you become an Approved User

There are two ways you can advertise jobs on EDon, either as an Approved User or an Unapproved User.

Approved User

Each time you notify a job vacancy you will have the option to become an Approved User. Approved Users:

  • save time when completing online job notification forms, as these will be pre-populated with your company details
  • can view, copy and amend job vacancies they have notified in the last 12 months

You use an ID and password to login and notify vacancies.

Unapproved User

As an Unapproved User you'll have to select the 'Notify a job' link on the login page, to advertise a job vacancy – there's a 'Help' link to guide you.

Until you register as an Approved User, each time you notify a job vacancy you will also have to:

  • complete the online job notification form in full
  • call our contact centre team to make any amendments to the job details

Users of EDon should frequently check for updates to the service, or more information. See the page in this guide on online advertising service user updates.

You can advertise a job now using the Jobcentre Plus online service.

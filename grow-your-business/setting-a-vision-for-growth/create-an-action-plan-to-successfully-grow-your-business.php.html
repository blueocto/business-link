Introduction

Most businesses need to grow in order to survive - but growth needs to be planned carefully. If you want to grow your business, this interactive tool can help by providing you with a tailored growth action plan and report.

It will ask you questions about your business and:

  • rate your performance in different areas
  • highlight opportunities for improvement
  • provide you with a list of actions to help build a strategy for growth

It should take under ten minutes to complete the questions (you can save your progress at any time and return later).

Identifying, protecting and transferring your assets

Introduction

There are two main types of business assets - tangible, physical assets such as plant and machinery, and intangible assets, such as your business' brand or know-how.

This guide explains how you can identify, value, protect and manage your business' assets. It outlines the main types of tangible and intangible assets, and describes the growing importance to businesses of knowledge-based - or intellectual - assets. It also includes information on transferring and selling assets.

The guide aims to help you to find the best ways to use your business' assets.

An innovation start-up

Introduction

An innovation start-up is a new business dedicated to successfully exploiting a new business idea. This is usually done by an entrepreneur - an inventor with personal ownership of an idea and a business plan of how to exploit it.

Sometimes, an idea may originate from within an existing organisation - eg a business or university. In these cases, the new business is called a 'spin-out'.

This guide shows you how to create an innovation start-up. It looks at how to set up a business and introduces the different types of finance that might be available. It also explains how investors make a return on their investments.

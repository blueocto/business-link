Managing your design projects

Introduction

Combining a design project with your business strategy can help you reduce costs and make sure you meet the required quality standard for your products or services.

This guide outlines some of the ways of managing design projects effectively. This includes how to plan and record the design process, the investment that may be required to implement design management effectively and the benefits of integrating design into the development process from conception to delivery of a new product or service.

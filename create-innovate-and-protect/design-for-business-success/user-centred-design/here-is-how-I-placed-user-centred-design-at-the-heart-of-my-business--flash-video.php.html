Here's how I placed user-centred design at the heart of my business (Flash video)

Argos is a general-goods retailer founded in 1973. It has over 700 stores throughout the UK and Republic of Ireland, and primarily displays goods in a catalogue, which is released twice each year.

Sunita Yeomans is the Design Manager at Argos. Here, she talks about how Argos use customer research and user-centred design in their business, and how it has changed the way the business works.

Argos

Sunita's top tips:

  • "Be careful not to pre-empt what your end-users are after and allow for the difference between what customers want and what they say they want."
  • "Be careful not to follow research blindly - use your own skillset to interpret the information and insight of your research."
  • "Engage with your end-users at all stages of development, not just at the beginning or at the end."

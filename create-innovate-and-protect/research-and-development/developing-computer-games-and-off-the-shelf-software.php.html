Developing computer games and off-the-shelf software

Introduction

The UK's software, video games and interactive entertainment industry is one of the biggest in the world. Gaming is one of the country's fastest growing sectors with technology, brands and products under constant development.

This guide outlines the software and gaming industries, providing information and links for individuals wishing to set up a games or software company, or for companies aiming to break into the games or software industries.

It also sets out how to carry out research and development, translation and localisation of games and software, how to protect your intellectual property (eg copyright), marketing and selling your product and regulation in the software and gaming industries.

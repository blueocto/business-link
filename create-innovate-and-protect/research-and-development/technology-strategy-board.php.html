Technology Strategy Board

Introduction

One challenge for any business is to successfully compete within rapidly changing local and global markets. Innovation can help your business create opportunities from current and future economic and social demands.

The Technology Strategy Board is committed to helping businesses meet the challenges of the future through innovation and to promoting a national culture where innovation can flourish.

In this guide, you will find information on why innovation is important for business and economic growth. You will also find details that explain how the Technology Strategy Board works and how you can get involved with them and networks of innovators. This guide also provides information on research and development competitions, research initiatives and programmes that can help with funding to enter international markets.

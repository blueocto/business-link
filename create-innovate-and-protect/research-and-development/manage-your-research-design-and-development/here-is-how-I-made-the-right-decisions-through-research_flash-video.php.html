Autoglass is a vehicle-windscreen repair company based in Bedford. It is part of the Belron Group, the world's largest vehicle glass repair and replacement company.

Dr Chris Davies is the Head of Technical Research and Innovation for Belron Technical. Here, he talks about how research and development help decision making when developing new products and processes.

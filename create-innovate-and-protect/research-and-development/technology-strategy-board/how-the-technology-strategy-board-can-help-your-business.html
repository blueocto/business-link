Technology Strategy Board

How the Technology Strategy Board can help your business

The Technology Strategy Board works in all sectors of the UK economy, and aims to work with organisations of any size that are committed to innovation and growth.

By working with the Technology Strategy Board, your business can access various services and advice, and receive support and potentially investment to suit your specific needs. There are several ways in which you can work with the Technology Strategy Board, including:

  • Joining a Knowledge Transfer Network (KTN) - national networks that bring businesses and academic organisations together to encourage innovation and knowledge transfer within a specific field of technology or business application. You can find out about KTNs on the Technology Strategy Board website - Opens in a new window.
  • Applying for a Knowledge Transfer Partnership (KTP) - to access academic research, knowledge, technology or skills in order to develop your business and encourage innovation. Read about KTPs on the Technology Strategy Board website - Opens in a new window.
  • Becoming involved in collaborative research and development (R&D) - the Technology Strategy Board aims to encourage businesses and research communities to collaborate in order to develop new products, processes and services. You can also enter one of the Technology Strategy Board's R&D competitions - see the page in this guide on research and development competitions.
  • Joining an international innovation programme to expand your business and consider innovation on a wider platform. For more information, see the page in this guide on international innovation programmes.
  • Participating in the Small Business Research Initiative programme - for more information, see the page in this guide on the Small Business Research Initiative.

Download 'What does your business need to innovate?' from the Technology Strategy Board website (PDF, 439K) - Opens in a new window.

You can also find information on driving innovation on the Technology Strategy Board website - Opens in a new window.

The Technology Strategy Board's _connect resource

The _connect platform enables online collaboration, networking and knowledge sharing. It allows you to find potential business partners, entrepreneurs, collaborators, researchers, academics and other innovators in your industry. You can join _connect on the Technology Strategy Board website - Opens in a new window.

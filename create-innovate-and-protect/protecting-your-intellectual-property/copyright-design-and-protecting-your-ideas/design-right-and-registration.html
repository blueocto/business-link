Design right and registration

Introduction

Design right - which is an automatic right - allows you to protect your designs in the UK and prevent others from copying or misusing them. You can protect the internal or external shape or configuration of an original design.

If you register a design, you may be able to strengthen and extend protection to other countries of any design right or copyright protection that may exist automatically. This means that in the event of an infringement action you would not have to prove that a design you had registered had been copied.

This guide explains the differences between design right and registration, and how your business can benefit from them. It covers the protection given by design right, explains how to register a design, and what to do if your design right or registration is infringed.

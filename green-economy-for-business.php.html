Green economy for business

The government's new publication on the green economy

'Enabling the Transition to a Green Economy: government and business working together' is a new publication which sets out what the transition to a green economy means for businesses.

It is the government's response to requests from the private sector for greater clarity on what government means by a "green economy", the policies being put in place to achieve this and how they come together.

The full suite of information available includes:

  • the government's vision - download the government's vision for Enabling the transition to a Green Economy (PDF, 582K) - Opens in a new window 
  • a timeline for the key policies and investments - download the policy timeline for Enabling the transition to a Green Economy (PDF, 292K) - Opens in a new window or download the approximate investments timeline for Enabling the transition to a Green Economy (PDF, 247K) - Opens in a new window
  • a document for SMEs on what the transition means for them - download a guide for small businesses on Enabling the transition to a Green Economy (PDF, 278K) - Opens in a new window 
  • 3 case studies highlighting how the chemicals, food and drink and automotive sectors are responding to the issues of moving to a green economy - download guidance on Enabling the transition to a Green Economy for the chemicals industry (PDF, 279K) - Opens in a new window, download guidance on Enabling the transition to a Green Economy for the food and drink industry (PDF, 255K) - Opens in a new window or download guidance on Enabling the transition to a Green Economy for the automotive industry (PDF, 408K) - Opens in a new window

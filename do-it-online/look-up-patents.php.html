Look up patents

Before you apply for a patent for your new product or process you should check that your invention has not already been patented or is not already known.

To get patent protection your invention must:

  • be new, not publicly known anywhere in the world prior to filing
  • have an inventive step, not obvious or a simple adaptation or combination of existing products
  • be capable of industrial application, having a technical effect

If you would like further information on whether you need to apply for a patent, see our guide on how to get patent protection for your business.

You can find information on granted patents and pending patent applications by searching the patents register.

  • Search the Intellectual Property Office online patents register by patent number

For further information, you can contact the Intellectual Property Office Information Centre on Tel 0300 300 2000 between 09.00 and 17.00 Monday to Friday, excluding bank holidays, or by email at information@ipo.gov.uk.

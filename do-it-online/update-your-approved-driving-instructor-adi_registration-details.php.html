Update your approved driving instructor (ADI) registration details

You can use this service to:

  • update your contact details
  • show your details on 'find your nearest driver instructor'
  • commit to the voluntary code of practice and doing at least seven hours of continuing professional development each year
  • view your check test history

You can use it between 06.00 and midnight, Monday to Sunday.

What you will need

You will need your:

  • online services user ID
  • password

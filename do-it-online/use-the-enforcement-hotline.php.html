Use the Enforcement Hotline

The Insolvency Service Enforcement Hotline enables people who have information about defiant directors and bankrupts to pass it on. The intention is to enforce disqualification and prevent the public from being exposed to more instances of corporate misconduct.

The Insolvency Service is the government agency which administers and investigates the affairs of bankrupt individuals through its network of official receiver offices.

Before you make a complaint, please ensure you have read our guide complain against an undischarged bankrupt or disqualified company director. This guide explains the grounds under which a complaint can be made.

Further guidance

  • Director disqualification
  • Sanctions on behaviour in event of a bankruptcy

For general insolvency enquiries, you can call the Insolvency Service Enquiry Line on Tel 0845 602 9848. Alternatively, you can send an email to insolvency.enquiryline@insolvency.gsi.gov.uk.

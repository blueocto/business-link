Apply for your first approved driving instructor (ADI) registration certificate

You can use this service to apply for your first approved driving instructor (ADI) registration certificate.

You can use it between 6.00 am and midnight, Monday to Sunday.

Payment

Payment will be taken from your account as soon as your application has been received. If your application is refused, the Driving Standards Agency (DSA) will give you a full refund.

If you have a photocard licence

You can tick a box to give the DSA permission to use your DVLA photo instead of sending one to DSA. You will need:

  • your driver number
  • your online services user ID
  • your password
  • a valid credit or debit card to pay the fee

If you do not have a photocard licence

If you do not have a photocard licence, or do not want the DSA to use the DVLA's photograph, you will have to send a recent passport sized photograph to DSA. You should write your PRN clearly on the back.

You will need:

  • your online services user ID
  • your password
  • a valid credit or debit card to pay the fee
  • your personal reference number (PRN) - this is also known as your ADI number
  • a printer

When you have completed the screens, you will need to print a copy of the final page. You can do this using your browser print option. Send the final printed page and a passport sized photograph to DSA at the following address:

Driving Standards Agency
The Axis Building
112 Upper Parliament Street
Nottingham
NG1 6LP

You can get help by phoning the DSA Helpline on Tel 0300 200 1122 or emailing them at adireg@dsa.gsi.gov.uk.

The DSA aims to process your application within ten working days.

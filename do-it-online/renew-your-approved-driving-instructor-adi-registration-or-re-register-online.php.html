Renew your approved driving instructor (ADI) registration or re-register online

Before you start

You can use this service to:

  • renew your approved driving instructor (ADI) registration
  • re-register as an ADI if your registration has lapsed

You can use it between 06:00 and midnight, Monday to Sunday.

Payment

Payment will be taken from your account as soon as your application has been received. If your application is refused DSA will give you a full refund.

How to use the service

Please make sure that you select the correct ADI certificate options. Choose from:

  • renewal
  • re-registration

If you have a photocard licence

You can tick a box to give DSA permission to use your DVLA photo instead of sending one to DSA. You will need:

  • your driver number
  • your online services user ID
  • your password
  • a valid credit or debit card to pay the fee

If you do not have a photocard licence

If you do not have a photocard licence, or do not want DSA to use DVLA's photograph, you will have to send a recent passport sized photograph to DSA. You should write your PRN clearly on the back.

You will need:

  • your online services user ID
  • your password
  • a valid credit or debit card to pay the fee
  • your personal reference number (PRN) - this is also known as your ADI number
  • a printer

When you have completed the screens, you will need to print a copy of the final page. You can do this using your browser print option.

You should then send the final printed page and a passport sized photograph to DSA at the following address:

Driving Standards Agency
The Axis Building
112 Upper Parliament Street
Nottingham
NG1 6LP

You can get help by phoning the DSA Helpline on Tel 0300 200 1122 or emailing them at irdt@dsa.gsi.gov.uk.

The DSA aims to process your application within ten working days.

Apply to start the approved driving instructor (ADI) qualifying process

Before you start

You can use this service to apply to start the approved driving instructor (ADI) qualifying process for the first time. You can use it any time between 06.00 and midnight, Monday to Sunday.

What you will need

You will need:

  • your driving licence number
  • your criminal record disclosure number, and the date the disclosure was produced
  • details of any motoring or non-motoring offences, and disqualifications from driving
  • details of any pending court cases brought against you

For further information you can see our section on becoming an ADI.

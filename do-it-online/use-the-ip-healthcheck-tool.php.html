Use the IP Healthcheck Tool

Have you thought about brand names, business names, inventions and designs you are developing? Do you know how to manage, protect or exploit them?

The Intellectual Property Office, the government body responsible for intellectual property (IP) in the UK, is offering a free diagnosis.

Its new IP Healthcheck tool gives your business a free and confidential IP report that:

  • takes less than 10 minutes to complete
  • is simple and intuitive
  • offers practical and useful advice to support your business
  • covers patents (including licensing), trade marks, designs and copyright

You can find further information on IP in our guide on protecting Intellectual Property.

For further information, you can contact the Intellectual Property Office Information Centre on Tel 0300 300 2000 between 09.00 and 17.00 Monday to Friday, excluding bank holidays, or by email at information@ipo.gov.uk.

Please note these services may be closed at certain times for maintenance and that between 01.00 and 03.00 (UK time) there are scheduled housekeeping tasks which may affect the availability of these forms.

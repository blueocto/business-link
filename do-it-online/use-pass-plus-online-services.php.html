Use Pass Plus online services

You can use this service to:

  • register to be a Pass Plus instructor
  • order Pass Plus refill packs
  • track if your pupils' Pass Plus certificates have been issued

You can use it between 6.00 am and midnight, Monday to Sunday.

What you will need

You will need:

  • your online services user ID
  • your password
  • a valid credit or debit card to register or order refill packs

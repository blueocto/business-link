Search for government opportunities on Contracts Finder

Contracts Finder is a free new service from businesslink.gov.uk. You can use it to search for procurement opportunities with government, including lower-value contracts worth £10,000 and above.

Government departments and their agencies are using Contracts Finder to publish live opportunities, as well as tender and contract documents for closed procurements. Many local authorities and other public sector bodies will join them in the future.

To find out what you can do with Contracts Finder, see our guide on how to find government opportunities, tenders and contracts on Contracts Finder.

You can use Contracts Finder to search or browse for live opportunities, closed tender or contract documents, or both. Registered users can also sign up for email alerts when new documents are published.

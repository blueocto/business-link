Search the Individual Insolvency Register

You are about to start an online search of The Insolvency Service's Individual Insolvency Register. The Insolvency Service is the government agency which administers and investigates the affairs of bankrupt individuals through its network of official receiver offices.

The Register contains:

  • details of bankruptcies that are either current or have ended in the past three months
  • current individual voluntary arrangements and fast track voluntary arrangements
  • Debt Relief Orders
  • current bankruptcy restrictions orders and undertakings

Related guidance

For instructions on why you would want to use the register and how to use it, see our guide on the Individual Insolvency Register.

See our guidance for further information on insolvency.

For general insolvency enquiries, you can call the Insolvency Service Enquiry Line on Tel 0845 602 9848. Alternatively, you can send an email to insolvency.enquiryline@insolvency.gsi.gov.uk.

Get started with company incorporation for agents

Introduction

If a client asks you to handle the incorporation of their business or to change its legal status, there are various options available.

This guide gives outline information on the structures available to UK businesses, and shows you where to find information on setting up and registering different types of business. It also includes advice on how to help your client choose the most appropriate structure for their particular needs.

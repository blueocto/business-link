Complain against an insolvency practitioner or The Insolvency Service

Taking your case to the Ombudsman

You can request a member of parliament to ask the Parliamentary and Health Service Ombudsman (PHSO) to investigate your complaint.

You cannot complain to the PHSO directly. The PHSO is independent of government and can also investigate complaints about the Adjudicator's Office.

You can find information on the role of the Ombudsman on the PHSO website - Opens in a new window or download guidance on how to complain from the PHSO website (PDF, 1.54MB) - Opens in a new window.

You can also ask for information by writing to the PHSO office at:

The Parliamentary and Health Service Ombudsman
Millbank Tower
Millbank
London
SW1P 4QP

Alternatively, you can call the Parliamentary and Health Service Ombudsman Enquiry Line on Tel 0345 015 4033 or email them at phso.enquiries@ombudsman.org.uk.

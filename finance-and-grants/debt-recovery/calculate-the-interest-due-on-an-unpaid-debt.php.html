Calculate the interest due on an unpaid debt

Introduction

You are entitled to charge interest on outstanding debts. You have a statutory right to claim interest on late payments. This is calculated using reference rates, which refer to the Bank of England base rate for a given period.

Alternatively, you may have decided to set a contractual rate that might be higher or lower than the statutory rate. If you have set a contractual rate, the statutory rate no longer applies.

Interest should be charged on the outstanding gross amount, inclusive of VAT. No VAT is chargeable on the interest itself.

This interactive tool will help you calculate the amount of statutory or contractual interest you are entitled to charge.

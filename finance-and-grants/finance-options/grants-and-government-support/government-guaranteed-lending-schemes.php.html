Government guaranteed lending schemes

Introduction

Finding business finance can be difficult, but in challenging financial times securing working capital for business development and growth is vital.

There are a number of government guaranteed financial schemes available to help viable businesses with temporary cashflow problems.

This tool can help you by providing an assessment of your eligibility for these types of finance schemes.

Go straight to the first question.

This tool provides an assessment of your eligibility for government guaranteed lending schemes. It does not recommend specific products and services and is not a substitute for professional advice. If you want to find other sources of finance and support search our business support finder for grants, loans, expertise and advice for which your business may be eligible.

This tool should take no more than five minutes to complete.

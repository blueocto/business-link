Drew McDonald
Oscar and Fitch

Drew's top tips:

  • "You should always be aware of where your next loan repayment will be coming from - ideally, this will be from the profitability of your business."
  • "If your project fails then the person who hurts the most should always be you - not a lender. And if you're frightened of that kind of responsibility then don't take out a loan."
  • "Don't give up if you get knocked back when applying for a loan. Keep trying and learn from your experiences."

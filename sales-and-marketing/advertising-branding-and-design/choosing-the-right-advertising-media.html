Choosing the right advertising media

Introduction

When you're advertising, you want to choose the most cost-effective method of getting your message to the right people.

This tool is designed to help you make that choice. It asks you a short series of questions about:

  • your product or service
  • the target audience for that product or service

You will then be directed to guidance on the advertising media most likely to help your business - providing you with a series of options to help you develop your own media plan.

It is not intended to be a substitute for professional advice and will take you no more than three minutes to complete.

Michael Jenkins
Up And Running NI Limited

Michael's top tips:

  • "Look at segments, so it's not just one big market you are looking at."
  • "Talk to your customers and listen to your customers as opposed to being stuck in an office."
  • "Try to guage how your business viewed externally and you will get a lot of interesting feed back."
  • "It is very important your staff are trained and developed so they understand customer needs."

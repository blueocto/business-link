Enabling the Transition to a Green Economy - a resource-smart economy

Introduction

Natural resources need to be used sustainably to ensure businesses are able to grow in the long term.

Resource efficiency presents huge opportunities to UK business, with government research showing potential savings of around £23 billion per year from simple measures.

The government's overall goal is to move towards a 'zero waste' economy, where resources are fully valued, used efficiently, and nothing of value is thrown away.

The government is taking action to ensure we use resources in a smarter way, such as removing barriers to energy efficiency under the Green Deal.

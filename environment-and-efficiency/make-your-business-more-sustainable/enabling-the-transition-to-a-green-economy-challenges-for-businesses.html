Enabling the Transition to a Green Economy - challenges for businesses

Introduction

Successful businesses will need to meet the challenges associated with the transition to a green economy and that result from climate change.

For example, a potential significant challenge for energy intensive businesses will be the increased costs as a result of climate change and other environmental policies, and from the increased cost of energy.

Some of the sectors most affected will be those needed for the supply chains for green products. The government will work together with businesses to enable them to seize green opportunities, minimising the cost of the transition and maximising opportunities.

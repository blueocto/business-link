Enabling the Transition to a Green Economy - opportunities for businesses

Introduction

The move to a green economy will affect different industries in different ways - and to varying degrees. This will create opportunities and challenges for businesses.

Successful businesses will need to adapt to take advantage of the benefits and opportunities, and to manage risks.

This guide focuses on what cross-sector and industry-specific opportunities there are for businesses to profit from the move to a green economy.

Assess your environmental compliance

Introduction

These tools will help you find out how to:

  • comply with environmental legislation and assist in avoiding prosecution and fines
  • save money by using your resources more efficiently
  • improve your business' competitive edge


How to use the tools

Each tool will take you through a series of questions about your business.

Your answers will generate a report containing environmental guidance relevant to your business. This will include guidance on the legislation that you must comply with and the permits, licences and exemptions that you may need. We recommend that you save the results of each tool before you start another.

  • Start the hazardous substances assessment
    Oil, chemicals, pesticides, solvents, ozone depleting substances, F-gases, radioactive substances
  • Start the emissions to air assessment
    Burning fuels, burning waste, fuel oil, boilers, furnaces, kilns, nuisances, climate change.
  • Start the equipment assessment
    Vehicles, electrical equipment, batteries, air-conditioning equipment, refrigeration equipment, fire protection equipment
  • Start the packaging assessment
    Packaging manufacturers, packing or filling products, importing packaging, branding packaging, reprocessing and exporting packaging
  • Start the waste assessment
    Storing waste, transporting waste, duty of care, hazardous/special waste, food and catering waste, animal by-product waste, radioactive waste.
  • Start the water use and efficiency assessment
    Discharges to sewer, trade effluent, surface water run-off, groundwater, water abstraction, impounding water.

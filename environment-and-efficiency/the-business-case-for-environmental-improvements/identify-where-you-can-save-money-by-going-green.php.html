Introduction

While it's possible for a business to save money at the same time as helping protect the environment, it is important to identify exactly where savings could be made.

This interactive tool will help you to assess the areas where you are most likely to be able to cut costs, based on your business activities. It will provide you with money-saving tips and links to sources of further information.

Using an environmental consultant

Introduction

Environmental issues are becoming significant for all businesses as the amount and scope of environmental legislation increases. Stakeholders such as employees, customers, suppliers and investors are also becoming more aware of, and interested in, the impact that businesses have on the environment.

There is a great deal of free information available for businesses that want to improve their environmental performance. However, if you are unsure what environmental legislation you need to comply with or what standards you need to meet, or you need more specialised advice, think about hiring an environmental consultant.

This guide looks at the ways in which using an environmental consultant could benefit your business. It tells you where to find consultants, outlines what to ask when choosing one and gives tips on how to manage the relationship.

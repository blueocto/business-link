Dealing with land contamination

Contaminated land environmental legislation

This page provides links to the full text of key pieces of environmental legislation relating to contaminated land. The websites hosting the legislation may list amendments separately.

If you are setting up an environmental management system (EMS) for your business, you can use this list to start compiling your legal register. Your legal adviser or environmental consultant will be able to tell you if other environmental legislation applies to your specific business.

For more information about EMS, see our guide on environmental management systems (EMS) - the basics.

  • Environmental Protection Act 1990. Establishes the legislative framework for identifying and dealing with contaminated land. Find the Environmental Protection Act 1990 on the Legislation.gov.uk website - Opens in a new window.
  • Contaminated Land (England) Regulations SI 2006/1380. Sets out provisions relating to the identification and remediation of contaminated land, identifies sites requiring regulation as 'special sites' and adds land contaminated by radioactive substances to this classification. Find the Contaminated Land (England) Regulations 2006 on the Legislation.gov.uk website - Opens in a new window.
  • Environmental Damage (Prevention and Remediation) Regulations SI 2009/153. Brings into force rules requiring polluters to prevent and repair damage to water systems, land quality, species and their habitats and protected sites. Find the Environmental Damage (Prevention and Remediation) Regulations 2009 on the Legislation.gov.uk website - Opens in a new window.
  • Radioactive Contaminated Land (Enabling Powers) (England) Regulations SI 2005/3467. Outlines the system to be used for identifying and remedying land contaminated by radioactive substances. Find the Radioactive Contaminated Land (Enabling Powers) (England) Regulations 2005 on the Legislation.gov.uk website - Opens in a new window.
  • Radioactive Contaminated Land (Modification of Enactments) (England) Regulations SI 2006/1379. Outlines measures for identifying and remedying contaminated land in situations where the operator of a nuclear installation is not liable under the Nuclear Installations Act 1965. Find the Radioactive Contaminated Land (Modification of Enactments) (England) Regulations 2006 on the Legislation.gov.uk website - Opens in a new window.
  • Radioactive Contaminated Land (Modification of Enactments) (England) (Amendment) Regulations SI 2007/3245. Amends 2006/1379 to make modifications made to Part 2A of the Environmental Protection Act apply to all radioactivity. Find the Radioactive Contaminated Land (Modification of Enactments) (England) (Amendment) Regulations 2007 on the Legislation.gov.uk website - Opens in a new window.
  • Radioactive Contaminated Land (Enabling Powers and Modification of Enactments) (England) (Amendment) Regulations SI 2010/2147. Amends 2005/3647 and 2006/1379 by redefining 'substance', removing the exclusion for radon and its decay products where they are the result of the after effects of a radiological emergency or past activity. Find the Radioactive Contaminated Land (Enabling Powers and Modification of Enactments) (England) (Amendment) Regulations 2010 on the Legislation.gov.uk website - Opens in a new window.

Find other current environmental legislation that may affect your business on the NetRegs website - Opens in a new window.

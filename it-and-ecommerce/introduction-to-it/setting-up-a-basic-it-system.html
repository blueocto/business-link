Setting up a basic IT system

Introduction

If you're starting a new business or you feel that you can benefit from using IT and e-commerce in your business, you may come across confusing or conflicting information on the best computer equipment to buy.

This interactive tool will identify the main ways in which you will use the new equipment, and who will be using it, and assess whether a simple setup is best for your business or whether a more sophisticated system may suit you more.

You will then be able to approach an IT retailer with more confidence about the basic specifications of new equipment and any peripherals that may be useful to your business.

Go straight to the first question.

You can save your progress while using this tool. To do so, you will need to log in or register - then you can come back and check or change your responses at any time, and the tool will give you updated advice. Please ensure that before saving, you have answered all questions on the page you are working on.

This tool is not a substitute for professional advice.

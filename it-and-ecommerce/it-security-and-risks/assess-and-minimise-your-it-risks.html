Assess and minimise your IT risks

Introduction

It is vital to recognise the risks associated with using IT in a business environment. These can range from accidental damage caused by employees to deliberate attempts from outsiders to illegally access data held by your business. There are many ways in which your business can be compromised if you don't undertake careful analysis of your business practices and security systems.

This tool will help you understand the main dangers to your systems and information posed by insufficient safeguards and training, and what to do to minimise their effects on your business. It should take about five minutes to complete.

The information provided in this tool should not be used as a substitute for legal or professional advice.

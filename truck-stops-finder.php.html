Truck stops finder

Truck stops are vital rest areas for lorry drivers. This service will help you find places where you can take a break, and shows the facilities available at each stop. For more information, see our guide on how to find truck stops in England. If you manage a truck stop shown here and want to update your details, contact the Highways Agency Information Line on Tel 0300 123 5000.

Brett Bannister
Sportsshoes.com

Brett's top tips:

  • "Gain relevant experience outside of the family business and bring something new to the table to avoid replicating the leadership style of your relative."
  • "Establish a formal forum for communication after transferring control of the business so that family members have an opportunity to discuss their concerns outside of the day-to-day running of the business."
  • "Separate the emotion of the business from the business itself. Ultimately it's a place of work for your employees so be careful not to lower the tone by communicating with your family as if you're in private."

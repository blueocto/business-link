This page contains links to essential resources including useful guides, tools and case studies designed to help you start your business.

Starting a Business - The No Nonsense Guide

The Starting a Business - The No Nonsense Guide, produced in partnership with government departments, is designed in six easy-to-use modules so that you can select the ones that are right for you.

Module 1: Overview - provides a summary of the basic steps you will need to take in order to set up your own business. Download Starting a Business - The No Nonsense Guide. Module 1: Overview (PDF, 2.24 MB) - Opens in a new window.

Module 2: Is starting a business right for you? - provides information and activities to help you decide whether running your own business will suit you. Download Starting a Business - The No Nonsense Guide. Module 2: Is starting a business right for you? (PDF, 1.31 MB) - Opens in a new window.

Module 3: Planning your business - provides information on planning the practical and legal aspects of your business. Download Starting a Business - The No Nonsense Guide. Module 3: Planning your business (PDF, 1.15 MB) - Opens in a new window.

Module 4: Finding and keeping customers - covers researching the market, getting your business offer right and successfully selling to customers. Download Starting a Business - The No Nonsense Guide. Module 4: Finding and keeping customers (PDF, 1.15 MB) - Opens in a new window.

Module 5: Managing money - includes information on the key skills needed to organise your business finances and how to apply for funding. Download Starting a Business - The No Nonsense Guide. Module 5: Managing money (PDF, 1.82 MB) - Opens in a new window.

Module 6: Your business plan - collates the information that you wrote in the worksheets in previous modules into an essential or comprehensive business plan. Download Starting a Business - The No Nonsense Guide. Module 6: Your business plan (PDF, 817K) - Opens in a new window.

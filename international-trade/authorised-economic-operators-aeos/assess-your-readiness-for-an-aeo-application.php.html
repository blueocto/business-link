Assess your readiness for an AEO application

Introduction

The Authorised Economic Operator (AEO) certificate is an internationally recognised quality mark that can give your business access to certain simplified customs procedures and the ability to 'fast-track' your shipments through certain HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC) safety and security procedures.

If you decide to apply for AEO status, filling out the form can be time-consuming. Therefore, before you consider doing so you should use this interactive tool to assess your readiness to apply for AEO status.

The tool will assess your business on the basis of what you will need to demonstrate when visited by HMRC during the application process. It should take no longer than five minutes to complete and give you an indication whether your business is ready to apply for AEO status.

Go straight to the first question.

Note: if the tool indicates that an AEO application is likely to be unsuccessful, you may still apply if you wish to do so.

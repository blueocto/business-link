Getting ready to export to individual countries

Introduction

Identifying export opportunities in individual countries requires basic preparation and detailed information about the target market.

This tool highlights the rules and regulations around VAT, export licences and permits and trade preferences which may apply to your goods. The tool also directs you to detailed demographic and marketing information about the country you're considering exporting to. You will be asked just a few simple questions and the tool should take no longer than five minutes to complete.

Change of address, directors and more - what to report to Companies House

Introduction

Companies and limited liability partnerships are required to give Companies House information about their business, and in particular about changes which may affect other organisations and individuals. This guide explains what types of change you need to report, when you should do so and how to go about it.

This guide also includes links to specific forms that you can download to fill in and report changes, along with guidance about who needs to complete the forms and where to send them.

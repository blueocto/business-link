Starting a company or organisation and Corporation Tax

Using an accountant to deal with HMRC

You can appoint an accountant or tax adviser - known to HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC) as an agent - to deal with HMRC on your behalf when you start a new company or organisation and for all your other Corporation Tax affairs. For example, your agent can tell HMRC your company is active on your behalf. Ask your agent about this if you wish to know more.

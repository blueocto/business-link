Selling or closing your company and Corporation Tax

HMRC can wind up your company if you don't pay Corporation Tax

In extreme cases, where you continue not to pay your company's Corporation Tax, HMRC will apply to the court for a winding-up order to have your company closed down.

Find out how HMRC can close your company if you don't pay your Corporation Tax in our related guide.

Closing your company and Corporation Tax

There are several reasons why you may want - or be forced - to close down or wind up your company or organisation, such as:

  • you may be retiring or moving into full-time employment so you want to close down your company's operations
  • someone your company owes money to for some time - a 'creditor' - gets a court order to force you to close down your company. This process is known as 'winding up' a company or 'insolvency'
  • the members of your club, society or association may want to close down the organisation

This guide only covers the impact of closing down your company or organisation on your Corporation Tax affairs. You should contact Companies House, the Insolvency Service, your financial adviser or specialist for other information and advice.

Read more about winding up a company in our section on insolvency information for limited companies.

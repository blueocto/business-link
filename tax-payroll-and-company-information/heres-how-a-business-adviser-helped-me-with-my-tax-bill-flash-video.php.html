Will's top tips:

  • "Find a good tax professional - you can do this online or through one of the professional associations."
  • "Be aware of all of your tax deadlines - it remains your responsibility to meet those deadlines, even if you use the services of a tax adviser."
  • "Keep on top of your records on a regular basis - don't leave it to the last minute!"

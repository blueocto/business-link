Expenses and benefits record keeping

Introduction

It's essential that you keep records of any expenses and benefits you provide to your employees. You'll need these records to make sure that your forms P11D, P9D and P11D(b) are accurate at the end of the tax year, and to ensure that you and your employees pay the correct amount of tax and National Insurance contributions.

You also need to retain these records in case HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC) asks to see supporting paperwork if they have any questions about how you have accounted for the expenses and benefits you have provided to your employees.

Estimate your rates bill

Introduction

This tool gives you an indication of what your business rates bill might be for 2012/13, based on the information that you give.

However, any calculated figures provided are only estimates. Your exact liability can only be provided by your local authority when it sends you your rates bill. We accept no liability for the information provided by this indicator.

As well as your basic business rates liability, this tool is also able to estimate your bill if you are entitled to small business rates relief.

However, the indicator cannot calculate any entitlement to relief under the following schemes:

  • the transitional arrangements which phase in large changes in bills arising from the 2010 revaluation
  • charitable or community amateur sports club relief
  • rural rate relief
  • empty/unoccupied relief
  • hardship relief

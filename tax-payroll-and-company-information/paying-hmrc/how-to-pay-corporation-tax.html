How to pay Corporation Tax

Introduction

This guide offers a reminder of Corporation Tax payment deadlines and explains all of the available payment options.

HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC) counts all of the payment methods explained in this guide as electronic, apart from payments by post.

Paying electronically:

  • is safe and secure
  • gives you better control over your money
  • provides certainty about when your payment will reach HMRC
  • may lower your bank charges
  • avoids postal costs and delays
  • lets you pay at a convenient time if you use Direct Debit, Bacs Direct Credit, internet/telephone banking or debit or credit card over the internet

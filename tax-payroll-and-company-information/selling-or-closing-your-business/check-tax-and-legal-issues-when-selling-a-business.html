Check tax & legal issues when selling a business

Introduction

Our interactive tool will guide you through the tax and legal issues you need to investigate when selling all or part of your business.

Just answer a simple series of questions with yes/no answers. You will then be presented with a checklist, including links to further information.

The tool is not a substitute for professional advice. Most business owners find it helpful to seek professional advice when selling all or part of the business. But using the tool will help you to be much better informed when you meet with professional advisers, saving you money.

Company and LLP strike-off, dissolution and restoration

Introduction

This guide explains how you can apply for voluntary strike-off, dissolution and restoration of your registered business - a company or limited liability partnership (LLP). It outlines the reasons why you might want to act in this way, what you need to do, and who you need to inform.

The guide outlines the circumstances in which a business can be struck off the Companies House register without the directors' or members' consent, and what happens to the business' assets in that case. It also looks at the processes for restoration of a company or LLP either by court order or by administrative restoration.

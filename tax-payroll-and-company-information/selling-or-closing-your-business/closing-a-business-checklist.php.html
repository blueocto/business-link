Closing a business checklist

Introduction

Every business owner will want to think carefully before exiting their business. You need to explore all the options and then plan your exit fully.

This interactive tool tells you what you need to do if you are closing, selling or transferring the business.

It also sets out the basic steps involved in stock market flotation and business mergers and explores how insolvency might affect your business exit.

The tool should be used as a guide only and is not a substitute for professional advice.

Although the data you enter will be stored securely we recommend that you do not include bank account details and other sensitive information

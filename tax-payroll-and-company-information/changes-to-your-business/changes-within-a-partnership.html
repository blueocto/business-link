Changes within a partnership

Introduction

Whatever type of partnership you may be in, it is important to understand what you have to do if certain changes to the partnership occur.

For example, when an existing partner retires or dies, or when a new partner joins, normally the original partnership must be dissolved and a new one formed to carry on the previous business.

This guide explains how various changes can affect a partnership, and what you need to do when they happen. Note that the rules are different for general partnerships compared with limited partnerships and limited liability partnerships.

Record keeping (individuals and directors)

Introduction

If you're a UK taxpayer you should keep a record of the tax you pay each year and other records relating to your income and outgoings. You'll need these to help you complete your tax return or to answer any questions from HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC) about a return you have already completed.

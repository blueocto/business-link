Record keeping (self-employed)

Introduction

If you are self-employed you must keep business records such as your accounts, evidence of tax that's been paid and other records relating to your income and outgoings. You will need these to help you complete your tax return or to answer any questions from HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC) about a return you have completed.

You will need to keep your business records separate from and for longer than your personal records. Most businesses find that it helps to have a separate business bank account.

If you are looking for a very quick introduction to record keeping, for example because you are just starting out and want to know the things you need to keep, follow the link to the section in this guide 'Record keeping at a glance'.

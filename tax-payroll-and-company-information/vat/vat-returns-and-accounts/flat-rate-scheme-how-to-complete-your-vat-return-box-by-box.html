Flat Rate Scheme: how to complete your VAT Return box by box

Introduction

At the end of each VAT period - usually every three months - you need to complete a VAT Return. A return tells HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC) how much your flat rate turnover was for the period - and the amount of VAT you must pay to HMRC or the amount of VAT you can reclaim from HMRC.

This guide explains in detail how to complete your return if you're on the Flat Rate Scheme.

How to submit your VAT Return online

Introduction

Since April 2010 most VAT-registered businesses have been required to submit their VAT Returns online and pay any VAT due electronically. HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC) is, from 1 April 2012, extending this requirement to virtually all VAT-registered businesses.

This guide explains what you need to do to submit your VAT Return online and how to pay your VAT electronically. You (or your accountant, bookkeeper, or other agent) can opt to use either the free HMRC VAT Online software or commercial software.

Submitting your VAT Return online is quick, convenient, reliable and secure.

How to register for VAT

Introduction

To register for VAT you will need to fill in one or more forms and submit them to HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC) for approval. You will then receive a VAT-registration number and certificate.

Most applications for VAT registration can be completed online. All other VAT registration forms are available to download from the HMRC website and then can be printed, completed by hand and submitted by post.

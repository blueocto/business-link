Cancelling your VAT registration

Introduction

There are a number of situations in which you must cancel your VAT registration, such as if you stop making VAT taxable supplies. In some circumstances you can voluntarily cancel your registration, for example if you expect the value of your taxable supplies to reduce or if your taxable supplies are mainly zero-rated.

This guide explains how you should go about cancelling your VAT registration, and how you should account for VAT after your registration is cancelled.

Filing accounts with Companies House: dates and checklist

Introduction

Every company, whether it is trading or not, is legally obliged to keep accounting records.

If you are a trading or dormant limited company or limited liability partnership (LLP), you need to send a copy of your annual accounts to the Registrar at Companies House.

This guide explains when and how your annual accounts must be filed, including any exceptions to the rules.

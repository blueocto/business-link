Companies House annual return

Introduction

An annual return is a record of key company information which you must provide by a designated date each year. Annual returns are sent to Companies House where they are filed and made publicly available, so that anyone can verify the details and legitimacy of any UK company.

This guide explains how and when an annual return should be filed. It also explains who needs to file an annual return and how to avoid making mistakes.

This guide refers primarily to the annual return (form AR01) for companies. There is a separate annual return (LL AR01) for limited liability partnerships (LLPs) and much of what applies to companies applies equally to LLPs.

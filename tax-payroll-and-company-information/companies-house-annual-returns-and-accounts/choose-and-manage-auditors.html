Choose and manage auditors

Introduction

This guide is for managers and owners of both limited companies and limited liability partnerships, and explains the role and responsibilities of an auditor.

The guide explains the reasons why it is necessary to appoint an auditor, how to choose one, how to manage the relationship between the business and the auditor, and how to remove an auditor if you are not satisfied with their performance.
